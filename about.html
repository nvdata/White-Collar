About

The Brazil-U.S. 40 and Under White Collar Lawyers Initiative is an organization founded in 2019 comprising attorneys under the age of 41 focused on government investigations and related litigation and compliance issues involving U.S. and Brazilian interests.

What are our goals ?

The objective of the Initiative is to bring together young attorneys based in both countries to share ideas and experiences, report on current trends, and develop a network of experts in this field.

This is an important initiative for the development of new lawyers.

The Initiative carries out its mission through its core programs, which include meetings and sessions with guest speakers (including government officials, defense counsel, and compliance executives), and publications dedicated to relevant topics.
