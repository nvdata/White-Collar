Boards

The Initiative features an 11-person Board comprising members of the broader Initiative. The Board is responsible for spearheading the work of the Initiative by organizing the meetings and sessions, structuring and maintaining this website, coordinating or otherwise promoting group publications, and exploring additional opportunities to further the Initiative’s work and mission..
